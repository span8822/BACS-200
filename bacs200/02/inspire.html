David Bowie

What makes a hero? A hero is someone that we look up to. Heroes embody what we truly value most. Therefore who we select as role models says a lot about who we are as people.

The heroes that I most admire have the following characteristics.

  • Outrageously courageous - they disturb the status quo and define a new normal
  • Motivate others - people follow those who inspire
  • Big footprint - impact on millions and change the world
  • Sacrifice & commitment - their impact usually costs them everything
  • Integrity - they live true to their calling

Out of all of these characteristics, I do not know of any other man that exemplifies them. David Bowie was and still is a musical inspiration. Not only that, he was a fasion icon. His daring choices not only ostracized him from a major group of society, but it allowed the younger generation to feel more accepted in their choices, whether it be personality wise or clothing wise. He helped shape a portion of his generation and more.
