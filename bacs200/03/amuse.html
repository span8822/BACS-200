Amusement #1: Foxes

Foxes amuse me. They are adorable animals that are very crafty and energetic. Whenever I look at videos of domestic foxes and see the havoc they wreak upon their owners, I don't want one, but I do giggle. I used to want a fox, but then I realized my lifestyle doesn't fit the time and energy a fox needs.

Amusement #2: Animation

Animation is such a hard art to succeed at. Not only does it take your ability to do art into consideration, it also takes the ability to recognize proportions and see how a body or thing moves in real life and translate it into a cartoon form. I can't even draw a stick figure. These people amaze me.

Amusement #3: Wood Carving

Wood Carving is absolutely fascinating! You're taking a hunk of wood and some tools and turning the hunk of wood into something that someone wants! It could be something useful or purely decorative. I absolutely love the idea of this. My uncle used to be big into woodworking, until he cut off his left thumb and index finger. That made it a bit hard to woodwork afterwards.
